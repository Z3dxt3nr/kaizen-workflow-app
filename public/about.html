About Kaizen Workflow

Continuous improvement made simple, measurable, and effective.

🌱 Our Mission

Kaizen Workflow is designed to make continuous improvement accessible for every team. From production to administration, this platform helps track, visualize, and enhance workplace efficiency through structured improvement tracking and data insights.

🛠️ Core Features

  • 5S Audit Tool for workplace organization assessments
  • Real-time Idea Tracker to log and monitor Kaizen improvements
  • Visual Dashboard for progress, completion rates, and 5S scores
  • Department and category-based performance analytics
  • Simple, local-first data storage (no internet needed)

🚀 Future Updates

Planned updates include:

  • Multi-user collaboration & cloud sync
  • Advanced analytics with exportable reports
  • Department performance benchmarking
  • Optional Premium Add-on tools for Safety and Quality tracking
